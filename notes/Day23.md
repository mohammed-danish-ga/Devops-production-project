# Day 23 - Kubernetes Networking and Services

- Learned how Kubernetes networking works between pods and services.
- Understood the role of Services in providing stable networking and load balancing.
- Studied ClusterIP, NodePort, and LoadBalancer service types.
- Learned how Kubernetes DNS resolves service names to ClusterIP addresses.
- Understood how applications connect to databases using service names instead of IPs.
- Gained production-level understanding of service discovery in Kubernetes.
